The place was very spacious, but parking is definitely a problem... When you enter, you would immediately know that it's a Chinese restaurant.

We used the function room at the second floor of the restaurant-- private, big, with good acoustics... Good for meetings...

We were served soup, shrimps, 2 kinds of fish, beef with broccoli, sweet and sour pork, noodles, and fried rice... Can I just say that I absolutely love fried rice? I base my ratings of Chinese restos on their fried rice... So, it was a fried rice fail tonight... It didn't make me want to keep eating rice... Chowking's YC fried rice is actually better... So sorry, it's just an okay rating for me.
